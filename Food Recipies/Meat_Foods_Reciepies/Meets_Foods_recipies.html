(1) Beef Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 300g beef strips
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 2 tablesoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic (minced)
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over high heat.
  2. Add the beef strips and cook until brown.
  3. Add garlic and onion, then stir for 2 minutes.
  4. Add bell pepper and cook until slightly soft.
  5. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  6. Serve with rice or noodles.

Desctibtion:

Beef Stir-Fry is a color and flavorful dish packed with juicy beef strips and fresh vegetables. it is quick to make, full of texture, and perfect for anzone who loves a balanced, tasty meals.

(2) Meatballs in tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Instructions

  1. Mix beef, egg, breadcrumbs, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Form small meatballs.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry the meatballs until brown.
  4. Remove them and add onion to the same pan; cook until soft.
  5. Add tomato sauce and let it simmer.
  6. Add the meatballs back into the sauce and cook for 10 minutes.
  7. Serve with pasta or bread.

Desctibtion:

Meatballs in Tomato Sauce is a classic comfort dish made of soft, juicy meatballs simmered in a rich tomato sauce. It is simple, hearty, and perfect with pasta or fresh bread.

(3) Beef Kofta

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion (grated)
  • 1 tablespoon parsley (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 teaspoon spices mix

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix beef, onion, parsley, salt, pepper, and spices.
  2. Shape into long kofta pieces.
  3. Heat a grill pan and brush it with oil.
  4. Place the kofta and cook on each side until done.
  5. Serve with rice, bread, or salad.

Desctibtion:

Beef Kofta is a delicious Middle Eastern-style dish made with seasoned ground beef shaped into long, juicy pieces. It is smoky, aromatic, and perfect for family meals.
