(1) Grilled Lemon Garlic Fish:

Ingredients:

  • 2 fish fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon (juiced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika

Instructions:

  1. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Coat the fish fillets with the mixture.
  3. Heat a grill pan and place the fish on it.
  4. Cook for 3â€“4 minutes on each side until golden.
  5. Serve with rice or salad.

(2) Garlic Butter Shrimp:

Ingredients:

  • 300g shrimp (peeled)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Parsley (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Melt the butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add the shrimp and cook until pink.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  5. Garnish with parsley and serve.

(3) Creamy Seafood Pasta:

Ingredients::

  • 250g pasta
  • 200g shrimp
  • 100g calamari
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 cup cooking cream
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. Boil the pasta and set aside.
  2. Melt butter in a pan and add shrimp and calamari.
  3. Cook until they turn soft.
  4. Add cooking cream and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  6. Add pasta and mix well until coated.
  7. Serve hot.
